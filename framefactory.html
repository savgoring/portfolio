PROJECT OVERVIEW
This website was for a client who wanted to upgrade their old website design. It was outdated, the font was unreadable, and had 6 different pages. I decided to make a one page design, which is easier for their customers to navigate. The clients have alot of green in their store, so they requested to have it in the website. Therefore, I did a dark grey, white and green color scheme. The clients loved the design, and I am in the process of developing it for them.


ROLES Prototyping, Wireframing, user-testing, UI/UX design

TOOLS Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
