in10words Story
Sharing Website
PROJECT OVERVIEW
In10words is a web based platform for users to share a story that fits within the daily category. The plot twist here is that the story has to be in 10 words or less. My team member and I did this because it makes it unique and blunt, where other websites usually allow 240 characters. The user can share their story on the website, with friends, or on their social media.
