PROJECT OVERVIEW
This project was created for a photoshop class assignment. The requirement was to re-design a movie or tv series poster. I had recently just finished watching stranger things season 1, which was my inspiration for this project. I took the elements and characters that played the biggest roles throughout the show and implemented them. I chose a dark blue monochrome theme, because it matches the shows gloomy/mysterious feel.

ROLES Ideation, graphic design

TOOLS Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop
