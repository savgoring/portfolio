Magazine Design
PROJECT OVERVIEW
This is a digital magazine featuring the most eye catching murals of the Vancouver Mural Festival in 2016 and 2017. The magazine is filled with information about the festival, who the artists are and the history and inspiration of each piece. I made sure the design could fit a strong visual accompanied by sections of large text body on each page. I aimed for a lively and bright theme with this project.


ROLES Wireframing, color scheming, researching, graphic design

TOOLS Adobe Indesign, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD
