PROJECT OVERVIEW
This logo was made for a personal trainer. The name EndorFit comes from "Endorphins" which are produced by excersicing, which help bring about feelings of euphoria and general well-being. The client and I both agreed that the strongest business elements of EndorFit are exercise, and a healthy heart. Which is why I chose to implement a heart with a heart rate, and weights.
