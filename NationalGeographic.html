PROJECT OVERVIEW
This was a project made in an Adobe InDesign course. The requirement was to find a national geographic cover you like, and re-design it as a digital magazine. During this project, I learned quickly how to design for text-heavy layouts and creative typography for editorial design. I created 4 pages in total, the homepage (shown) and three other pages -in both vertical and horizontal formats.


ROLES Graphic Design, Research

TOOLS Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign
