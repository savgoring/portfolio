DummyDial Mobile
Application
PROJECT OVERVIEW
DummyDial helps people politely remove themselves from unwanted or awkward situations by sending real calls and texts directly to the users phone. Myself and 3 other team members worked together closely and strategically to create the final product which is now available on the apple and google play store. My main role was the design, and I had to ensure that the user could use the app easily in a hurry, and keep the design simple so it is descrete.


ROLES Ideation, concepting, wireframing researching, UI/UX design, graphic design

TOOLS Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, CSS/SASS
